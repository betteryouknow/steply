Frequently asked

Are the steps really free?
Yes. The full worked solution is always free, up to a daily limit. Premium adds unlimited solves, Learn mode, and Practice.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Steply → Cancel. You can also reach this from Settings inside the app.

How do I restore a purchase?
In the app: Settings → Restore Purchases.

Is it accurate?
Every answer is independently verified before it's shown. If a result can't be confirmed, Steply flags it honestly so you can double-check.

Is Steply a cheating tool?
No — it's a learning aid. Learn mode is built to teach you the method, not just hand over answers.
