Privacy Policy

Home · Terms · Support

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Steply ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we process and why. In short: we don't sell your data, we don't track you across other apps or websites, and we don't permanently store the math problems you submit.

Information we process

  • Math problems you submit (typed text, photos, or handwriting). These are sent to our AI provider solely to generate a solution and are processed in memory and then discarded. We do not keep your problem images on our servers.
  • On-device data: your solved-problem history, bookmarks, learning sessions, and practice sets are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to us.
  • Basic app preferences (e.g. onboarding status, selected topics, daily free-solve count) stored on your device.

How problems are solved

To produce step-by-step solutions, the text or image of your problem is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This data is used only to return your solution and is not used to identify you. Google processes it under its own terms; see Google's privacy documentation for details. Answers are independently verified before they are shown.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not require an account or ask for your name, email, or phone number to use the app.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not use advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell or rent your information.
  • We do not permanently store your submitted problem images.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through your App Store account. We never receive your payment card details. Apple handles billing, renewals, and refunds under its terms.

Children's privacy

Steply is a learning aid suitable for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app works without an account and does not request personal details.

Data retention

Submitted problems are discarded immediately after a solution is generated. On-device content remains on your device until you delete it (or uninstall the app).

Your choices

You can clear your history in the app at any time, and uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
